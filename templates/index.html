We Have Rooms's Like - Duplex , Deluxe Room ,Studio Room

A studio room offers all the amenities of a small apartment, plus a studio bed. This convertible bed can be turned into a sofa or another type of furniture. A studio room is ideal for longer-term stays.

General Suite

A General suite comprises two rooms on separate floors, with a private staircase or lift between them. A duplex is a good choice for a guest such as the business traveller who wants to work or hold meetings in one room and sleep in the other.

Major Suite

A Major room will generally be larger and more lavishly appointed than other types of hotel rooms. They will often have additional features such as a whirlpool bath, a private patio or balcony, or a particularly outstanding view. The cost will also be higher .
